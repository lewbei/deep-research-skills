# Decision Log

A chronological record of every major decision made during the workflow, plus the alternatives rejected and the residual uncertainty. This is the audit trail for the project.

## Entry format

```
### D<id>: <decision summary>
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Phase:** 1–10 (or "external" if not part of the loop)
- **Decision:** what was chosen or rejected
- **Trigger:** what event forced the decision (e.g., new evidence, failed probe, time budget threshold)
- **Evidence:** sources, probe results, or execution outcomes that supported the decision
- **Alternatives rejected:** what else was considered and why it was not chosen
- **Residual uncertainty:** what is still unknown after this decision
- **Consequences:** what this decision enables or blocks
```
