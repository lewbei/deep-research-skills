## Confidence / triangulation column

Use one of:

- `single-source` — only one source found; treat as provisional.
- `triangulated` — ≥2 independent sources corroborate the same claim.
- `empirically-validated` — the claim was tested by a probe script or direct execution.
- `official` — from the competition/platform itself (rules, leaderboard, docs).
- `unverified` — source is a forum/blog post/unstarred repo; may be wrong.
